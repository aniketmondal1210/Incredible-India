Savor Indian Cuisine

Butter Chicken

A rich and creamy curry made with tender chicken in a tomato-based sauce.

Masala Dosa

A crispy fermented crepe filled with spiced potatoes, served with chutneys and sambar.

Biryani

A fragrant rice dish cooked with meat or vegetables, aromatic spices, and herbs.

Palak Paneer

A vegetarian dish consisting of paneer cheese in a thick paste made from puréed spinach.

Vibrant Festivals

Holi

The festival of colors, celebrating the arrival of spring and the triumph of good over evil.

Diwali

The festival of lights, symbolizing the victory of light over darkness and knowledge over ignorance.

Onam

A harvest festival celebrated in Kerala with intricate flower carpets, exciting boat races, and grand feasts.

Durga Puja

A grand festival honoring the Hindu goddess Durga, celebrated with elaborate pandals, cultural events, and devotional activities.
